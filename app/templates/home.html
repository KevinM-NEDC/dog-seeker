Dog Seeker AI

Dog Seeker is an AI detection program that identifies the breed of a dog. To start, upload an image of a dog and the AI will do its best to identify from its registered 13 dog breeds

How Does it work?

Our AI was trained with over 6,000 labeled images of different dog breeds to accurately identify 13 of the most common dog breeds. Some images below provide an example of our models predictions.

13 Registered Dog Breeds

As of right now, we have taught the AI to recognize 13 dog breeds: Akita, Shiba Inu, Australian Shepard, Beagle, Cavalier King Charles Spaniel, Corgi, Dachshund, German Shepard, Golden Retriever, Husky, Pitbull, Basset Hound, and Rottweiler.
